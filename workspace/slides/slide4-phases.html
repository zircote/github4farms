Implementation: 4 Phases

Phase 1

4 weeks
Preparation & Analysis: Learner needs assessment, learning objectives, session outlines

Phase 2

6 weeks
Iterative Design: Prototype first 3 sessions, pilot testing, accessibility audit

Phase 3

8 weeks
Development: Complete 8-12 sessions, project templates, quick-reference guides

Phase 4

12-24 weeks
Delivery & Evaluation: Deliver sessions, collect Kirkpatrick L1-4 data, identify train-the-trainer
Week 4: Session outlines complete, learning objectives defined (Bloom's Taxonomy)
Week 10: First 3 sessions piloted, 90% accessibility compliance achieved
Week 18: All 8-12 sessions developed, materials fully accessible
Month 6: 75% learner completion, 60% active GitHub usage at 60 days
