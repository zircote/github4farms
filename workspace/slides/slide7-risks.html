Top 5 Risks & Mitigation Strategies

  • R-001: Technology Anxiety & Learner Resistance

    HIGH (9)
    Mitigation: Use agricultural analogies, celebrate small wins, normalize mistakes, share peer testimonials, apply adult learning principles (Andragogy)

  • R-002: Seasonal Availability Conflicts

    MEDIUM (8)
    Mitigation: Modular curriculum design, schedule around planting/harvest seasons, provide recap sessions after gaps, offer asynchronous video resources

  • R-003: Accessibility Compliance Failures

    MEDIUM (8)
    Mitigation: Implement UDL from start, use accessibility checkers (WAVE, axe DevTools), test with assistive technologies, target 90% WCAG 2.2 AA compliance

  • R-004: Single Point of Failure (Instructor Unavailability)

    MEDIUM (8)
    Mitigation: Develop comprehensive facilitator guides, record all sessions for asynchronous access, build buffer time in schedule, identify backup facilitator

  • R-005: Cognitive Overload (Content Too Complex)

    MEDIUM (6)
    Mitigation: Chunk content into 3-5 key concepts per session, slow pacing, use visual aids and job aids, apply Cognitive Load Theory principles
