Current State: The Problem

  • Digital Literacy Gap: Farmers lack GitHub and version control experience; many lack basic technical skills
  • Technology Anxiety: Non-technical learners resist or feel intimidated by developer tooling
  • Seasonal Availability: Planting and harvest seasons create training attendance gaps and knowledge decay
  • Mixed Device Access: Learners use varied devices (laptops, tablets, phones) with inconsistent internet
65-70% of farmers cite lack of technical skills as primary barrier to digital tool adoption (Industry Research)
