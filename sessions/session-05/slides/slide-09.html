Email Notifications

GitHub can also send notifications to your email.

To check your email settings:

  1. Click your profile icon (top right) then "Settings"
  2. Click "Notifications" in the left menu
  3. Make sure your email is listed under email preferences
  4. Choose what gets emailed vs. shown only on GitHub

Tip: If you check email more than GitHub, turn on email notifications. Otherwise, turn email off to avoid a flooded inbox.
