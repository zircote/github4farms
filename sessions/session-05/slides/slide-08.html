Your Notification Inbox

The bell icon is your GitHub inbox.

Click the bell icon (top right of any page) to see your notifications:

  • Unread notifications appear with a blue dot
  • Click a notification to go directly to the Issue, PR, or comment
  • Click "Done" to mark it as read
  • Click "Unsubscribe" to stop notifications for that specific thread
