Managing Notification Volume

Too many notifications? Here's how to manage them.

  1. Change your Watch level — switch to "Participating and @mentions"
  2. Unsubscribe from specific threads you don't need
  3. Filter notifications — show only Issues, PRs, or @mentions
  4. Adjust email settings — turn off email for low-priority items

Start with more notifications, then trim down. It's easier to turn things off than to miss something important.
