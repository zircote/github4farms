Watching a Repository

To start getting notifications, "watch" a repository:

  1. Go to the repository
  2. Click "Watch" (top right, near Fork and Star)
  3. Choose your level:
  • Participating and @mentions — Only when involved (default)
  • All Activity — Notified about everything
  • Ignore — No notifications
  • Custom — Pick exactly what you want

Recommended: Start with "All Activity" on your main farm repo.
