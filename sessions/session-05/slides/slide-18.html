Guided Practice Steps

Step 1: Watch the repository

  1. Go to the demo farm repository
  2. Click "Watch" then select "All Activity"

Step 2: @mention your partner

  1. Open any existing Issue, scroll to the comment box
  2. Type: "@[partner] What do you think about this task?"
  3. Click "Comment"

Step 3: Check your notifications

  1. Click the bell icon, find your partner's notification

Checkpoint: Both partners should have a notification.
