Guided Practice Steps

Round 1 — Partner A suggests, Partner B reviews:

  • Partner A: Fork the demo farm repo
  • Partner A: Edit crops/planting-schedule.md — change one date
  • Partner A: Commit with a message, create a Pull Request
  • Partner B: Open the PR, read changes, leave a comment
  • Partner B: Approve and merge

Checkpoint: Partner A's change should now appear in the original repository.
