From Board View to Roadmap View

In Session 3, you learned Board view and Table view — great for daily work, but they don't show when things happen across the season.

Today we're adding:

  • Custom fields — extra details like crop type, planting date, expected yield
  • Iterations — time blocks that divide your season
  • Timeline view — a visual calendar stretching across months

This is the difference between a to-do list and a seasonal plan.
