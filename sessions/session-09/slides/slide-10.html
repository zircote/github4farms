Switching to Timeline View

How to open Timeline view:

  1. Open your Project
  2. Click "New view" (the "+" next to the view tabs)
  3. Select "Roadmap" from the layout options
  4. Choose which Date field to use for start and end
  5. Your Issues appear as bars on the calendar

Navigation: Scroll left/right through months. Zoom in/out for weeks or quarters. Click any bar to open details. Drag bars to change dates.
