Evaluating Copilot's Output

After Copilot gives you a draft, ask three questions:

1. Is it ACCURATE? Are the facts correct? Steps in the right order?

2. Is it COMPLETE? Did it cover all the steps? Anything missing?

3. Is it RELEVANT to MY farm? Does it match my crops, animals, equipment, and local conditions?

Like reviewing a new farmhand's work. Read it over and make corrections.
