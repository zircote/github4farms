Five Tips for Better Prompts

  1. Be specific — Name the task, crop, animal, or equipment
  2. Give context — Farm size, location, or season
  3. Say what format you want — "Write a checklist" or "Create a step-by-step plan"
  4. Include key details — Dates, quantities, field names
  5. Ask for what's missing — "Include supplies needed" or "Add time estimates"

Like giving directions to a new farmhand. The clearer your instructions, the better they can help.
