What Copilot CAN Do

Copilot is good at:

  • Drafting Issue descriptions from a short prompt
  • Suggesting checklists and step-by-step task breakdowns
  • Filling in common details you might forget
  • Helping you organize thoughts into a clear structure
  • Writing faster than typing everything from scratch

Farm analogy: A farmhand who has read many farming books and can suggest what to include in your work orders.
