Viewing the Actions Tab

Where do you see your automatic helpers at work?

Every repository has an Actions tab. Click it to see:

Green Checkmark

Success — the helper finished the job correctly

Red X

Failure — something went wrong

Yellow Dot

In Progress — the helper is working right now

Farm analogy: Think of the Actions tab as the job board in the farm office. Each completed job has a green check (done right) or a red X (problem).
