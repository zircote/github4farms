The 4-Step Teaching Method

When teaching any skill, follow these four steps:

Step

What You Do

Farm Example

1. SHOW

Demonstrate while they watch

“Watch me start the tractor”

2. EXPLAIN

Talk through WHY each step matters

“I check the oil first because...”

3. LET THEM TRY

Hand over control, let them practice

“Now you start it”

4. GIVE FEEDBACK

What went well, what to adjust

“Great start — check mirrors first”

This is how experienced farmers have always taught. Today you'll use the same approach for GitHub.
