Capstone Project: The Big Picture

Your challenge: Build a complete farm repository from scratch.

Think of it as building a brand-new farm office. You need:

  • The building (repository)
  • The filing system (Issues and labels)
  • The planning wall (Project board with views)
  • The work order forms (Issue templates)
  • The schedule (iterations and Timeline view)
  • The AI helper (at least one Copilot-drafted Issue)
