Commenting on Issues

Comments let you add updates and discuss tasks.

To add a comment:

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the Issue page
  2. Type your update in the comment box
  3. Click "Comment"

Farm examples:

"Ordered the replacement post. Arriving Thursday."

"Fixed the fence. Used 3 new posts and 20ft of wire."

"Vet says vaccinations need to happen before May 1."
