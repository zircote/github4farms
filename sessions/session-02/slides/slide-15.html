Closing an Issue

When a task is done, close the Issue.

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the Issue
  2. Click "Close issue" (or "Close with comment" to add a final note)

A closed Issue:

  • Turns from "Open" to "Closed"
  • Stays in your repository forever (it's not deleted!)
  • Can be reopened if the task comes back
  • Becomes part of your farm's permanent record
