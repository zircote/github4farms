Writing a Good Title

Good titles are short and specific.

Bad Title

Good Title

"Problem"

"Fix broken fence post — north pasture"

"Tractor"

"Tractor oil change due — 500 hour service"

"Seeds"

"Order corn and soybean seeds for spring"

"Help needed"

"Irrigation leak in Field 2 — repair by Friday"

Rule of thumb: Someone should know what the task is just from reading the title.
