What Are Copilot Agents?

Agents = A Farmhand Who Follows Multi-Step Instructions on Their Own

Copilot helped you write one suggestion at a time. An agent handles a whole sequence:

  • Read through your repository
  • Identify what needs to be done
  • Create Issues, suggest changes, or organize tasks
  • Work through multiple steps without you guiding each one

Key difference: Copilot suggests one thing at a time. An agent follows a plan with multiple steps.
