Agent Exploration (Bonus)

Finished your Spark app early? Try this with your partner:

  1. Open Copilot Chat in your farm repository
  2. Try a multi-step request:
    • "Suggest which open Issues should be worked on first"
    • "Create three Issues for end-of-season tasks"
    • "Review my most recent PR and suggest improvements"
  3. Evaluate: Did the agent understand your request?

The agent proposes, you decide.
