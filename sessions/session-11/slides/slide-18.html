Independent Practice

Build a simple farm tool with Spark and evaluate the result.

Choose your scenario: A) Seed Calculator | B) Equipment Checklist | C) Livestock Log

  1. Describe: Write a prompt telling Spark what to build — 5 min
  2. Review: Test what Spark creates with real data — 10 min
  3. Refine: Ask Spark to fix or improve one thing — 10 min
  4. Compare: How does this compare to doing it manually? — 5 min
  5. Reflect: When would you use Spark on your farm? — 5 min
