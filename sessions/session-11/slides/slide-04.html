What Is GitHub Spark?

Spark = A Farmhand Who Builds Simple Tools from Your Description

Copilot helped you write text. Spark goes further — it builds working mini-apps.

  • No coding required — type what you need in everyday language
  • The result is a simple web app you use in your browser
  • You can share it with others on your farm

You describe what you want in plain English, and Spark creates a small, working tool.
