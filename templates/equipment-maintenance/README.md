### Step 1: Create Your Repository

1. Click **"Use this template"** (green button)
2. Name it (e.g., "equipment-log" or "farm-equipment-2026")
3. Click **"Create repository"**

### Step 2: Log Equipment Activity

**For scheduled maintenance:**
1. Issues â†’ New issue â†’ **"Routine Maintenance"**
2. Fill in: equipment name, service type, hours/mileage, date

**For unexpected repairs:**
1. Issues â†’ New issue â†’ **"Repair Request"**
2. Fill in: what's broken, urgency, photos of damage

**For periodic inspections:**
1. Issues â†’ New issue â†’ **"Equipment Inspection"**
2. Fill in: equipment checked, condition assessment, action items

### Step 3: Track and Close

- Add comments as work progresses (parts ordered, repair started, etc.)
- Attach photos of completed repairs
- Close the Issue when maintenance is complete
- The closed Issue becomes a permanent service record
