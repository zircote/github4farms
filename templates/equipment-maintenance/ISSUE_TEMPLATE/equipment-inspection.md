## Condition Assessment

### Safety Items
- [ ] Emergency shut-off functional
- [ ] Guards and shields in place
- [ ] Fire extinguisher accessible and charged
- [ ] Warning decals legible
- [ ] Seat belt functional (if applicable)
- [ ] Lights and signals working
- [ ] Horn/backup alarm working

### Mechanical Items
- [ ] Engine oil level adequate
- [ ] Coolant level adequate
- [ ] Hydraulic fluid level adequate
- [ ] Belts — no cracks or excessive wear
- [ ] Hoses — no leaks or bulging
- [ ] Tires — adequate tread and pressure
- [ ] Brakes — functional
- [ ] Steering — responsive, no excess play

### Structural Items
- [ ] Frame — no cracks or damage
- [ ] Cab/ROPS — secure and undamaged
- [ ] Implements — properly attached and secured
- [ ] Electrical connections — clean and tight

## Overall Condition
- [ ] Good — ready for use
- [ ] Fair — minor issues noted (see below)
- [ ] Poor — needs attention before next use
- [ ] Unsafe — do NOT operate until repaired
