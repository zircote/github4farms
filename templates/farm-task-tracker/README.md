## How to Use This Template

### Step 1: Create Your Repository

1. Click **"Use this template"** (green button at the top of this page)
2. Name your repository (e.g., "my-farm-tasks" or "smith-farm-2026")
3. Choose **Private** if you want only you to see it, or **Public** to share with your team
4. Click **"Create repository"**

### Step 2: Create Your First Task

1. Go to the **Issues** tab
2. Click **"New issue"**
3. Choose a template:
   - **Equipment Repair Request** â€” for broken or malfunctioning equipment
   - **Livestock Health Check** â€” for animal health observations or vet visits
   - **Field Inspection Report** â€” for crop conditions, soil issues, or weather damage
   - **General Farm Task** â€” for anything else
4. Fill in the blanks and click **"Submit new issue"**

### Step 3: Organize with the Project Board

1. Go to the **Projects** tab
2. Open the "Farm Task Board"
3. Add your Issues to the board
4. Move tasks between columns as work progresses:
   - **Todo** â€” Tasks that need to be done
   - **In Progress** â€” Tasks someone is working on
   - **Done** â€” Completed tasks

### Step 4: Invite Your Team (Optional)
